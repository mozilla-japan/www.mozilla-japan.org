Acknowlegement

author: dynamis

Same as all other open source works, localization is the collaboration work with contributers, bug reporters and QA testers. We sincerely appreciate their help and I hope all of you're enjoying your sweet fruits.
Though not complete, here is the list of cooperators' name in alphabetical order. If you have done well but not in this list or you want to fix the representation of your name, so sorry and tell us please...

Each list for the same product contains only names who contributed after the previous version.
